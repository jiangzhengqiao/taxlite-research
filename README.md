## Reports

### [2026 — Unclaimed state concessions](2026-unclaimed-concessions/)

**At least $356.9 million per year** in unclaimed state-administered energy and water concessions across NECF 5 states + Victoria's three-utility concession cluster.

First systematic Australia-wide dollar estimate. Independently corroborated for NSW by the state government's own annual report (within 1 percentage point).
